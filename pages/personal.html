Background

I hold dual citizenship between the U.S. (Dad's side) and the U.K. (Mum's side). I grew up in the Lower Hudson Valley of NY, and was lucky to spend many summers visiting family upstate in the Adirondacks region, out west in Oregon, and across the pond in England.

Hobbies

In my spare time, I really enjoy being active outdoors; I love soccer, volleyball, tennis, rock climbing, sailing, hiking, backpacking, camping, and roadtripping. I also enjoy cooking (& eating – especially spicy, vinegary, or umami foods), reading (fiction mostly), listening to music, playing cards, and film photography.
